# Dry run — rubric vs the eval-v4 boards (2026-07-22)

Purpose: prove the axes discriminate before the suite ever runs. Method: apply the two
render-judged axes (SQ, IC) to the five committed `eval-v4-*` boards exactly as
the axis rubrics (now `axes/`, then a single `axes.md`) specify — independent judge sessions, isolation rules enforced — and score
the results against ground truth (canvas JSON pulled from :4000). IF/ES/PH are not
render-judgeable retroactively without fixtures; their validity rests on their anchors,
every one of which is a real documented session from the round-1 corpus (see §4).

Judges: one SQ judge (saw the SQ rubric + the two reference PNGs + the five board PNGs,
nothing else — specifically not the v4r1 reports), one IC blind judge (saw ONLY the five
board PNGs), one IC scorer (this document's author, comparing the blind reconstruction
to the boards' canvas JSON). Renders: `preview.svg?fit=content&pad=48` at 2400px.

## 2. IC results

The blind judge produced full structured reconstructions for all five boards (archived:
`ic-reconstruction` output). Scored against canvas JSON with ad-hoc comprehension keys
(CORE = flow order, branch conditions, group membership, loop targets; SECONDARY =
labels, conventions, inventory), hedged recoveries at 0.5 per the amended rubric:

| board | R | corrupted | IC | what the board failed to transmit |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| eval-v4-flowchart | 1.00 | 0 | **10** | nothing — all 15 edges, 3 loops, fraud branch, sticky, conventions recovered outright |
| eval-v4-org-tree | ~1.00 | 0 | **9.5** | only the acting-edge source needed inference (orange wire overlaps the Marketing trunk) |
| eval-v4-swimlane | 0.88 | 0 | **7** | spans/poll chip-pairing "could be swapped"; status-wire origin ambiguous; write chip stranded at frame edge — 4 of 13 CORE facts hedged |
| eval-v4-state-machine | 0.84 | 0 | **6.5** | 5 of 12 CORE facts hedged: recovered-wire merges into handshake wire, packet-loss source obscured, grace-expired/resume-window converge, session-resumed origin ambiguous, heartbeat association by proximity only — the junction machinery tax, measured |
| eval-v4-nested-arch | 0.90 | 0 | **6.5** | Postgres visibly wired to nothing (both read/write relations pure inference); stock.changed attachment untraceable on the shared dashed trunk; publish directions inferred from port names, not arrows |

(nested-arch lands at 6.5 despite R 0.90 because three CORE relations — both
Postgres I/O paths and one publish — survived only as inference; the "every CORE fact
recovered [unhedged]" qualifier on the 7/8 rows governs.)

Zero corrupted facts across all five boards is itself a finding: v4's failure mode is
**ambiguity, not misdirection** — the boards make readers work, they don't lie. The old
system's anti-parallel-overlap boards would have produced corruptions (a back-edge
reading as bidirectional is a wrong-direction assertion waiting to happen); that
distinction is exactly what the corruption gate is for.

## 3. Do the axes discriminate? Yes.

- **Different spreads:** SQ spans 5.5–7.0 (1.5 pts); IC spans 6.5–10 (3.5 pts) on the
  same five boards. IC is not a re-render of SQ.
- **Different rankings:** flowchart is 2nd on SQ but a perfect 10 on IC (its dead frame
  is an aesthetic cost with zero communication cost). The state-machine loses only 0.5
  SQ to the flowchart but 3.5 IC points — the crosshair junctions barely dent "how it
  looks" and severely tax "what it transmits." Swimlane and state-machine tie at SQ 6.0
  for opposite reasons and then separate on IC (7 vs 6.5).
- **Convergent where they should converge:** nested-arch is last on both axes — a board
  that is both ugly *and* ambiguous scores like it.
- **Independent evidence chains:** the blind judge's UNCERTAIN lists independently
  rediscovered, from pixels alone, the exact defects the round-1 evaluators found from
  transcripts: the unanchored heartbeat badge, the junction-glyph discontinuities, the
  stranded spans/poll chips, the pill column's orphaned reading. The axes are measuring
  real properties of the artifacts, not judge idiosyncrasies.

## 4. IF / ES / PH standing

Not dry-runnable against these boards (no fixture checklists existed when they were
built; edit-session diffs weren't snapshotted). Their rubrics are anchored instead to
documented round-1 sessions — IF's caps to the silent-drop (old swimlane S6) and
declared-substitution (v4 heartbeat badge) precedents; ES's 2-anchor to v3 flowchart
S6's 13-object churn and its 10-anchor to v4 state-machine S3's 5-op surgical edit; PH's
9-anchor to v4's zero-failed-call build and its 2-anchor to the old system's 12–23-retry
syntax fights. First live suite run validates them end-to-end.

## 5. Amendments adopted from this dry run

1. **Hedged recovery = 0.5 credit** (folded into the IC rubric, now axes/ic.md). Without it, the blind
   judge's diligence would have laundered the state-machine's junction tax into R = 1.0;
   the UNCERTAIN section is where bad boards confess.
2. **Anonymize PNGs for the IC blind judge** (already in RUNNER.md §4): dry-run
   filenames leaked genre ("state-machine"), which can seed TOPIC. Serve `board.png`.
3. SQ sub-check lines proved worth keeping mandatory — the org-tree/flowchart gap is
   only legible through them (both "good boards", different failing sub-checks).
